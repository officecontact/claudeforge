## Copywriting
**AIDA** (landing pages): Attention > Interest > Desire > Action.
**PAS** (emails): Problem > Agitate > Solution.
**Rules:** Benefits over features. Concrete numbers. One CTA per piece. 8th-grade reading level.
**SEO:** Primary keyword in title/H1/first paragraph/meta. 150-160 char meta description.
